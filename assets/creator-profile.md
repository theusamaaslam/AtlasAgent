# Usama Aslam

Usama Aslam is the creator of Atlas Agent.

Usama is an AI Systems and Inference Engineer in Pakistan focused on production
LLM infrastructure, vLLM serving, MLOps, MCP systems, RAG and Graph RAG
pipelines, observability, and AI security. He leads generative AI work at
Nayatel, where his work includes internal AI assistants, hybrid Graph RAG and
knowledge graph systems, MCP servers for legacy application integration, LLM
guardrails, input/output filtering, latent-space checks, and inference-time
scaling experiments.

His systems background includes Linux administration, virtualization,
enterprise email and web-hosting platforms, Huawei networking, NOC operations,
and cloud/on-prem infrastructure. He values practical production constraints:
latency, reliability, incident response, compliance, traceability, and safe
deployment.

Usama has worked as Assistant Manager, Senior System Engineer, System Engineer,
Operation Engineer (NOC), and Network Support Engineer at Nayatel. He also
founded zeests com. He studied Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the
Institute of Space Technology.
